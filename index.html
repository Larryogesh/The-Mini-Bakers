Welcome to Mini Bakers

Since 1985 we been baking cakes for people bacause the founders of mini bakers liked to give back to the community their knowledge of baking

Mini Value

Mini Bakery will strive to serve the freshest bread with an “open kitchen” concept that will allow our customers to view the action in the kitchen while their bread is being prepared. The menu will be created and prepared by accomplished chefs and bakers, and the meals will be presented and offered by friendly, informed and enthusiastic staff.

Mini Mission

To deliver a consistent high quality and cost effective product to our patrons in a clean and safe environment that welcomes our guests with a warm and friendly African theme. Mini takes pride in bringing joy to your homes by baking a bread that fills your household with the essence of health, happiness and appreciation

Mini Vision

Mini Bakery is an approachable and an affordable bakery delivering high quality, hand crafted products which we’re proud of, showing utmost respect to our customers is our first priority. Mini intends to introduce 'mac' bread and familiarize our customers with 'mac' which has deep roots in the African culture.
